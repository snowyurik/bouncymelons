How to get listed

You are in charge for your extension, it's all yours, but we can help you to promote it. Jut send us the following information:

  • Extension name
  • Link to your project (you can use github, sourceforge or else for free hosting if you need, but it's probably better to create website, if you want to make some money in opensource world)
  • Author name or your company name.
  • Is it freeware or commercial
  • download link or zip archive with copy of your extension in case it's commercial. For us to test it.

mailto: melon.nosp@m.s@vi.nosp@m.hv.or.nosp@m.g

DISCLAMER: we won't publish your extension if it will broke any of our automated tests (use run-tests.sh to check yourself)

Also we have a right to hate you for no reason. Bouncy Melons are pretty sensitive!
